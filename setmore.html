Visa Ban Consultation

. Duration:10 mins··. Price:$100
We are offering a limited, discounted 10-minute consultation for $100 for individuals with questions related to the recent visa ban announcements and travel restrictions.

This brief consultation is designed for targeted, time-sensitive questions, including whether a particular country or visa category may be affected, how the ban could impact a pending or approved case, and immediate next steps to consider. It is intended to provide clear guidance and issue-spotting, not a full case strategy or document review.

Due to the fast-changing nature of visa bans and government guidance, this option allows you to speak directly with an immigration attorney and get reliable, up-to-date information before making any decisions.

Appointments are limited and available on a first-come, first-served basis.
